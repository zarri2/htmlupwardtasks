AMIR BioGraphy

Amir Khan is a Pakistani cricketer and philanthropist born on October 10, 1982, in Lahore, Pakistan. He began his cricket career at a young age and quickly rose through the ranks, making his international debut for Pakistan in 2003.

Amir is a left-arm fast bowler and left-handed batsman, known for his impressive speed and swing. He has taken over 200 wickets in international cricket and has been a key player for the Pakistan national team. Amir has also played for various domestic teams, including Lahore Qalandars in the Pakistan Super League.

In addition to his cricket career, Amir is also recognized for his philanthropic work. He has supported various charitable organizations and has been involved in several initiatives to promote education and healthcare in Pakistan.
